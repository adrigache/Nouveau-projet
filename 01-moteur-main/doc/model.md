## 1 cash flow
### description
Cash flow is the movement of money in and out of a business.
It tracks actual cash received and cash paid during a period.
It is divided into three parts:

Operating cash flow: Cash from day to day business activities
Investing cash flow: Cash used for or generated from investments such as equipment or assets
Financing cash flow: Cash from loans, investors, or payments to shareholders

## 3 balance sheet
### description
It is a financial statement that shows a company’s financial position at a specific point in time.
It is structured as:
Assets: What the company owns
Liabilities: What the company owes
Equity: Owner’s residual interest

Core equation: Assets = Liabilities + Equity
